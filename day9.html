  • Allama Iqbal

    Allama Iqbal (1877-1938) was a renowned South Asian Islamic philosopher, poet, and politician. Born in Sialkot, Punjab, India (now in Pakistan), he was a descendant of a Kashmiri Brahmin family that had converted to Islam in the 17th century.
